About Me

I am a freelance data scientist where I specialise in using advanced analytics and machine learning algorithms to make sense from substantive amounts of data which can guide decision making and improve business performance. I have experience in airport analytics, human resource analytics, natural language processing and churn modelling. In terms of a specific tech stack, I favour Python & SQL/SparkSQL, but I am comfortable working in R as well. I also have experience with both AWS & GCP environments.

Previously, I graduated top of my class with a first class honours MSc of Business Analytics from Michael Smurfit Graduate Business School. Prior to that, I graduated from UCD with a first class honours BComm degree specialising in Business Analytics and Finance, where I was also awarded an academic achievement scholarship in the final year of my undergraduate degree.

On-Time Performance Forecasting

I am currently working on a project with Dublin Airport to develop machine learning models to forecast the on-time performance of departing flights. The intended application of these models is two fold: Firstly, the models will provide insight into what the most important factors are impacting on daily level and flight level OTP. Secondly, by tweaking input values to the forecasting model it will be possible to carry out scenario testing and what-if analysis for days in advance.

Carpark Time Series Forecasting

On a previous project I was tasked with developing a multistep time series forecasting model for carpark entries and exits. The model was developed using historical data and involved intricate feature engineering to accurately forecast entries and exits for the next 48 hours. This allowed the operational staff to develop rosters and schedule breaks more intelligently and reduced staffing costs.

Churn Classification Modelling

I developed a neural network classification model to identify customers likely to churn. As a precursor to the modelling work, I carried out a deep dive analysis of the key factors impacting on churn rate and presented the findings to business stakeholders.

HR Analytics & Attrition Modelling

While contracted to improve AIB's attrition rate, I developed machine learning models to forecast staff which were likely to leave the company. The benefits of this modelling work were two fold: proactively identify staff likely to leave and attempt to intervene, and statistically understand what the most critical factors are impacting on attrition rate, allowing the business to make more informed strategic decisions to retain employees in the long term.

Social Media Sentiment Analysis Modelling

I completed a contract in one of Ireland's major banks where I was tasked with building a state of the art sentiment analysis machine learning model to analyse customer sentiment from social media channels towards the brand, allowing the business to pivot away from the traditional paper based surveys. I developed and tested a number of machine learning models such as logistic regression, SVM, MLP neural network and LSTM neural network. The LSTM neural network, as the best performing model, was deployed and a live streaming Tableau dashboard was developed for ease of understanding across the business.

Gambling Analytics

I've worked on gambling analytics and premier league results prediction as a personal side project over the last number of years. I managed to develop forecasting models with levels of accuracy comparable to leading bookmaker, Paddy Power.
